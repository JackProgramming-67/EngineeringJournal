Initial Planning and Design

Rules and requirements

The RoboQuest challenge consists of two teams, Blockers and Shooters. Blockers aim to ram and chase the Shooters, while the Shooters aim to find and knock over the target in the center of the field. The field consists of a black rectangle with a red circle in the center, and another black square inside the red circle.

Main objectives & plan

As a shooter, my main objective is to knock over the target in the center. My plan is to allow the robot to detect black and red, then define functions for each colour.

Features and behaviours

The robot features turning a random rotation between 90 and 180 degrees when black is seen, and for red the robot stops, rotates until the target is seen with the ultrasonic sensor, then shoots.
